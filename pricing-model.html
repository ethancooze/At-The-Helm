Pricing Rules — Non-Negotiables
🚫

Never discount day rate

Reduce scope or days instead. Discounting signals lack of confidence in value.

✅

Always minimum 3-month commitment

Embedded work takes time. One month doesn't get results. Protects both sides.

🚫

No free discovery calls beyond 30 mins

Diagnostic is the paid entry point. Free calls are fine, but free work isn't.

✅

Invoice monthly in advance

Not on completion. In advance. Sets the right tone and protects cash flow.

💡

Anchor to the cost of inaction

"What is this problem costing you every month?" — always more than the retainer.

💡

Helm discount for embedded clients

Offer 3 months free Helm as part of transition out. Locks in SaaS revenue.
