The problem

Growth creates chaos. Someone has to fix it.

As businesses scale, operations break. Processes built for £5m don't work at £20m. Founders end up running the business instead of building it. Teams lose direction. Nothing gets owned.

The answer isn't another consultant with a PowerPoint. It's someone who embeds into your team and actually fixes it.
